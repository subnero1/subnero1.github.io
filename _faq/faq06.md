Subnero modems are available in four editions: Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Bronze. Silver is the all-round commercial, research, and defense option, and Gold is the higher performance variant with greater compute capacity and support for up to four receive channels. Platinum is the MIL qualified variant for demanding operational environments, and Bronze is a cost optimized platform for early stage testing and education.

For more information about the different editions, visit: [https://subnero.com/products/#smart-modems](https://subnero.com/products/#smart-modems)
