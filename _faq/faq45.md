For the Standalone configuration, the standard aluminium hull is rated to 300 m. Hull upgrades are available for deeper deployments: stainless steel to 2000 m and titanium to 4000 m. The Open configuration does not carry a depth rating of its own, since depth protection is provided by the host platform it is integrated into.

For more details, refer to Subnero's [hull options](https://subnero.com/products/wnc/gen4x/accessories/hull-options.html).
